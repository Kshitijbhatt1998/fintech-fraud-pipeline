Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Fraud Detection Pipeline project ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you visit our landing page or use the analytics dashboard.

1. What This Project Is

The Fraud Detection Pipeline is a portfolio / research project demonstrating an end-to-end machine learning pipeline for financial fraud detection. It uses the publicly available IEEE-CIS Fraud Detection dataset — no real customer financial data is processed.

2. Information We Collect

  • Analytics events — if PostHog is configured, we collect anonymous page-view and interaction events (e.g., which dashboard tab you visited). No personally identifiable information (PII) is attached to these events.
  • Authentication data — if you create an account via Supabase, your email address and encrypted password hash are stored by Supabase in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We do not store passwords ourselves.
  • Error reports — if Sentry is configured, anonymized error stack traces are sent to Sentry to help us fix bugs. PII transmission is explicitly disabled (send_default_pii=False).
  • Log files — server-side logs record timestamps and anonymized session events for debugging. Logs are rotated and deleted after 30 days.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and control access to the dashboard.
  • To debug application errors and improve reliability.
  • To understand aggregate usage patterns (anonymous analytics only).

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and user storage (privacy policy)
  • Sentry — error monitoring (privacy policy)
  • PostHog — product analytics (privacy policy)

5. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you request deletion. Analytics and error data are retained for 90 days. Log files are rotated after 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below. For Supabase-stored data, you may also submit a deletion request directly through Supabase.

7. Security

Passwords are hashed by Supabase using bcrypt. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. The analytics dashboard is read-only and serves no real financial transaction data.

8. Children's Privacy

This project is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

10. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Open an issue on GitHub.
